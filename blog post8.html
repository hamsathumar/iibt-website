Celebrating Success: IIBT Campus at the 7th Convocation Ceremony of Gatehouse Awards UK

Posted on: April 18, 2025 | Author: Hamsath Umar

A significant milestone was achieved on Saturday, 12th April 2025, as the students of IIBT Campus took center stage at the 7th Convocation Ceremony of the Gatehouse Awards UK Qualifications, held at the prestigious Bandaranaike Memorial International Conference Hall (BMICH).

The ceremony was conducted under the distinguished leadership of Mr. Sainulabdeen Najimudeen, the Country Director of Gatehouse Awards - UK in Sri Lanka. It was a proud moment for IIBT Campus, as 50 of our students successfully completed their Higher National Diploma in Quantity Surveying and were formally awarded their certificates in front of an esteemed audience.

Adding further prestige to the event, Attorney-at-Law Mr. Atham Lebbe Aazath, Chairman of IIBT Campus, graced the occasion. A highlight of the ceremony was the special recognition bestowed upon Mr. Aazath, who was honored with the Gatehouse Awards UK Membership Award, presented by Mr. Najimudeen.

The event was not only a celebration of academic success but also a reflection of the dedication, perseverance, and excellence that IIBT Campus strives to instill in all its students. It was a day filled with pride, emotion, and inspiration — a fitting tribute to the hard work of both the students and the academic team behind their achievements.

At IIBT Campus, we remain committed to empowering future leaders in Quantity Surveying and other professional fields, providing them with the skills, qualifications, and global recognition necessary to succeed in a competitive world.

Congratulations once again to all our graduates! We look forward to witnessing your continued success in the industry.
